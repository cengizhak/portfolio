About

Investigative by nature,
rigorous by practice.

I'm a UX designer focused on research, with a strong foundation in both qualitative and quantitative methods. I use research to uncover what users actually need — and to guide design decisions based on evidence, not assumptions.

I'm drawn to the investigative side of design: conducting interviews, designing studies, and analyzing results to produce clear, actionable insights. I enjoy turning complex findings into practical improvements in usability.

I prefer going deep into problems — identifying patterns, building understanding, and then shaping effective solutions. I'm comfortable both creating new concepts and refining existing systems.
